$$\TR$$ | Real numbers | $$-3, 1, 1.5, \pi, ... $$
$$X^d$$ | Set of all $$d$$-tuples of $$X$$ | $$\setb{0, 1}^2 = \setb{(0, 0), (0, 1), (1, 0), (1, 1)} $$
